Curriculum Vitae

About Me

I was a post-doctoral fellow in the Mathematics Department at the University of British Columbia. I obtained my PhD degree at Dalhousie University in August 2021, under the supervision of Theodore Kolokolnikov.

Research

My research focuses on dynamical systems and mathematical modeling, with an emphasis on pattern formation in reaction-diffusion systems using asymptotic and singular perturbation techniques.
